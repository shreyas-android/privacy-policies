Safety Standards – Child Protection

AIPulse is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on AIPulse:

  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming, exploitation, or abuse of children
  • Content that promotes or depicts harm to minors

Reporting Content

Users can report posts and content that violate these safety standards directly within the app. Reported content is reviewed, and content that violates these policies may be removed.

Enforcement and Legal Compliance

If content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation is identified, it will be removed immediately. Appropriate action will be taken, including reporting to relevant regional or national authorities when required by law.

Age Requirement

AI Threads is intended for users aged 18 years and older. Users under the age of 18 are not permitted to create an account or use the app.
