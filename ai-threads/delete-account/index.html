What we will delete

When you request account deletion, we will remove:

  • Your authentication record (email/login)
  • Your user profile (display name, avatar)
  • All content you uploaded or created (posts, images, videos, comments)
  • Associated metadata (user id mapping, preferences, saved items)

Note: Some aggregated or anonymized analytics data and logs may be retained for debugging and legal purposes in a non-identifying form.

What we may keep and retention periods

  • Backups: We may keep encrypted backups for up to 30 days after deletion requests to allow recovery in case of accidental deletion. Backups are removed as part of our regular retention schedule.
  • Legal records: If required by law (e.g., for investigation), limited records may be retained for the minimum period required by law.

Verification & Processing time

To protect your account, we will verify deletion requests by confirming the email address associated with the account or by requiring a valid authentication token. After verification we will:

  • Begin deletion processing within 48 hours.
  • Complete deletion of live data within 30 days (excluding backups/legal retention noted above).

Partial data deletion / export

If you want certain data removed (e.g., specific posts or uploaded images) without deleting your account, contact us at the same email above and specify which items to remove. We can also provide a copy of your data on request (data export) — include "EXPORT" in your request.
